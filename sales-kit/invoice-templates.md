# Sp33dy.net Invoice Template Pack

Use these templates in Stripe (Option A/B) after a client accepts an offer.

---

## 0) Default Billing Policy (Operational)

- Project work: **50% upfront / 50% before final handoff**
- Milestone work: each milestone billed and paid before milestone execution
- Retainers: billed monthly in advance
- Default terms: **Due on receipt** (or Net 7 if needed to close)
- Late fee reference: **1.5% monthly** (or max lawful rate)
- Currency: USD unless otherwise agreed

---

## 1) One-Time Project Invoice (High-Ticket)

### Invoice subject
`Sp33dy.net Project Invoice — [Client Name] — [Project Name]`

### Invoice description (paste)
`Scope: [Project Name]. This invoice covers [Deposit 50% / Full Project / Final 50%] for agreed deliverables. Includes strategic planning, execution assets, and delivery support per approved scope.`

### Suggested line items
1. `Discovery + Strategy` — Qty 1 — $[ ]
2. `Build/Execution: [Deliverable]` — Qty 1 — $[ ]
3. `Final Delivery + QA` — Qty 1 — $[ ]

### Payment terms note
`Payment due on receipt. Work begins once deposit is paid. Final files/implementation handoff delivered after final payment.`
